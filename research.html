My research focuses on the application of cutting-edge technologies across various computational domains. The core areas of my work include:

Core Research Areas

  • **Cloud Computing**: Exploring scalable and secure cloud architectures.
  • **Cyber Security**: Focusing on data protection, network security, and cryptographic methods.
  • **AI & ML (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning)**: Developing and applying machine learning models for data analysis and predictive systems.
  • **Data Science**: Utilizing data mining and analytical techniques to extract insights.
  • **IoT (Internet of Things)**: Designing and implementing secure, health-monitoring, and recognition-based IoT systems (as demonstrated by patents).

Current/Recent Projects

Based on published work and patents, current projects focus on:

  • **Smart Health Monitoring Systems**: Implementing wearable and IoT-enabled devices for real-time health data collection and monitoring.
  • **Biometric and Recognition Technology**: Developing systems like the IOT-Enabled Face Mask for security and monitoring.
  • **High-Performance Web Access**: Investigating optimized database solutions (e.g., using MongoDB) for efficient web applications in sectors like banking.

Prospective Students & Collaboration

I am actively seeking motivated students and collaborators interested in any of the above core areas. If you are interested in a thesis or research project related to Cyber Security, IoT device development, or AI-driven Cloud systems, please send an email with your CV.
